About Me

Hello World! I've spent the last seven years in government contracting (negotiating fixed-price and cost contracts with large defense companies; developing requests for proposals; writing recommendations for claims associated with defective pricing; overseeing source selections; and signing contracts for IT h/w and s/w, technical services, and weapon system development, production, sustainment, and civil works contruction).

Prior to this profession, I was active duty Air Force in a program/project management careerfield. Whether it was modernizing h/w and s/w for sattelite tracking stations, managing a third-line maintance program for the Canadian Air Force's fleet, working as an action offer supporting a 3-star general's pomp and circumstance events, or working on DoD and DoS task orders in the Green Zone - these were extraordinary career broadening opportunities.

I have a business background and a bit of a lifestyle entrepreunrial spirit. I decided to participate in Columbia's Full Stack boot camp because I was ready for my next challenge. Of course, it would be quite rewarding to graduate and be skilled enough to assist vetreprenuers with their Full Stack development needs. Stay tuned...
